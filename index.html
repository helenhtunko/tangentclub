Tangent Club

Founded in 2025 – A space for ideas, questions, and great conversations.

Our Objectives

  • To help young people understand more about what's happening in the world and important problems in society.
  • To get more people involved in talking and sharing ideas, both in planned meetings and casual chats.
  • To help members think more deeply and make good choices by looking at things from many different points of view.
  • To foster effective and intellectual thoughts among students and the community by understanding many social and global topics.

Activities

  • Every few weeks (1 or 2), the club meets up and chooses a topic — from "how pineapple on pizza changed the world" to "why companies are diving deep into social media advertising."
  • We meet up again to discuss the topic the next week.
  • Discussions, debates, presentations, and creative artwork are all welcome.

Rules & Guidelines

  • All opinions are respected.
  • Everyone can respectfully agree or disagree with each other's opinions.
  • Everyone can share anything.
  • Group discussions will follow after each presentation or creative work presented.
  • One person speaks at a time (but can politely interrupt if necessary).
  • You don't have to be formal — just be yourself.
  • Any problems or difficulties will be solved with the power of friendship.
  • Anyone can communicate in any language they're comfortable with, as long as it's semi-formal and suitable.
  • Collaboration is key, and everyone must be willing to participate and converse.
